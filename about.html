M/S Jagdish Mistry & Associates began its creative existence with Architect Jagdish Mistry in the year 1986. M/S Jagdish Mistry & Associates portfolio covers all major sectors. Our work encompasses both architectural and interior design. The practice is dedicated to the deepest level of involvement in the design of their buildings in order to deliver projects which meet the highest possible standards of excellence. The company’s work is characterized by strong conceptual legibility, innovation and a rigorous approach to detailing, all underpinned by the principles of humane, enduring and sustainable design.

Today, we have successfully completed over approximately 50 Corporate offices, 15 Showroom Interiors, and about 85 Residential Projects, along with some prize winning Exhibition Stalls across India.

Design Philosophy

Jagdish Mistry & Associate’s philosophy is firmly rooted in the belief that success is the result of the collaboration of good design & strategic planning. The study of people’s well-being lays the foundation for our strategic planning and design. We begin with: LIFE – THEN SPACE AND FINALLY BUILDINGS. Everything we produce from buildings to an interior space is characterized by its legibility. Our designs are innovative, they often surprise but are always precisely tuned to the requirements of the client. The ability to not only accept, change and alteration but to use these critical events in a productive and reinvigorating manner is central to the progressive practice of our firm.

Architectural Design

We believe that Architecture is not only limited to design of Built structure, instead it is about creating sustainable spaces where function has precedence over form. The design is developed in totality while considering all the spheres of architecture i.e. planning, form development, landscaping, services, interiors etc.
